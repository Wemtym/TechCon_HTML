Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a grassroots initiative to bring tech professionals together to discuss emerging trends in software, hardware, and innovation. Over the years, it has grown into a global platform attracting thousands of attendees from all over the world.

Our Mission

At TechCon, we aim to inspire innovation, facilitate meaningful collaboration, and promote inclusive access to cutting-edge technologies. We believe in empowering the tech community to build a smarter, more sustainable future.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Ada Thompson

AI ethics researcher and author of "Humane Algorithms," Dr. Thompson challenged the industry to think more critically about responsible innovation.

Ravi Patel

Co-founder of GreenStack, Ravi spoke on climate-focused computing and the role of tech in sustainable infrastructure.

Elena Grace

A pioneer in robotics and automation, Elena highlighted the transformative power of machine learning in industrial systems.
